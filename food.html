BlackJack Cafe
BlackJack is a small cafe on Club Road in New Delhi, India. Some say it's one of the best cafes in Delhi for food, and that it has the best burgers and hot dogs in town. Others say it's a budget-friendly cafe. BlackJack serves fast food, burgers, and beverages. Some say it offers many different types of fries and milkshakes.
Location: 1 23 Club Road West Punjabi Bagh Club Road, N W Ave Rd, Delhi, 110026
Avg Cost : Rs 350 (for two people)
Uncle's Sip & Bite
Uncle's Sip & Bite located at Prashant Vihar is a nice and cozy restaurant serving amazing and delicious fast food items and some refreshing shakes and drinks. Everything is freshly prepared and so good that you will crave for more. The ambiance of the restaurant is simple yet attractive and vibrant. It is a great place to hang out with your friends and grab a quick bite. Uncle's Sip & Bite is a pocket-friendly restaurant and offers home delivery also.
Location: A-75, Shop no. 2,4 & 5, Prashant Vihar Opposite CRPF school, Delhi, India 110085
Avg Cost: Rs 300 (for two people)
Cafe Russmunch
An amazing spot to hangout in the company of friends, this cafe offers plenty of options to soothe the sweet as well as savoury cravings. The menu includes Continental and Fast Food preparations, which can be available for pretty pocket-friendly prices. Being a cafe, there is an extensive menu of beverages available as well, which are one of the highlights of the joint. Recommended: burgers and shakes.
Avg cost : Rs 500 (for two people)
Location : 8, Chanderlok Enclave, Pitampura, New Delhi
BTW
Bittu Tikki Wala offers a delightful culinary experience with their exceptional tikkis. The crispiness of the tikkis and the burst of flavors from the chutneys create a perfect harmony. The hygiene standards are commendable, ensuring a safe and enjoyable dining experience. Bittu Tikki Wala truly stands out for its consistency in delivering delectable, authentic tikkis that keep customers coming back for more. It's a must-visit for anyone craving a satisfying and flavorsome street food adventure.
